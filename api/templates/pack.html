How it Works

1

Account Creation

  • Signup at Buyboxexpress.com. Go to prep service of client portal
  • Enter the description of the goods and the tracking number of the shipment that you have sent to our address

2

Inspect and Unpack

  • We receive your goods
  • We inspect the quantity and the condition of the goods
  • We will update the condition of the received goods in your portal

3

Preparation

  • Creat shipping plan and send us FNSKU lables as 30 per page pdf
  • We will prep and send you dimensions and weight of boxes

4

Shipping

  • You will buy shipping lables
  • We will print out the shipping labels, put them on the packed shipping boxes and handover to a shipping carrier in Canada or USA
